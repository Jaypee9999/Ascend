Program Overview

ASCEND is a flagship initiative under the Board of Investments' (BOI) Academe-Industry Matching (AIM!) Program. Developed in strategic partnership with the Samahan sa Pilipinas ng Industriyang Kimika (SPIK), ASCEND is designed to bridge the gap between higher education institutions (HEIs) and the Philippine chemicals industry in fostering long term, structured collaborations that drive innovation, workforce development, and sustainable industrial growth.

At its core, ASCEND empowers academia and industry to co-create solutions, and align research with real-world industry needs. Through strong partnerships, ASCEND helps cultivate a talent pool equipped with relevant skills and promotes research that directly contributes to the competitiveness of the chemicals sector which helps ensure that education and research are aligned with evolving industry needs.
