About SPIK

Samahan sa Pilipinas ng mga Industriyang Kimika (SPIK) is the Chemical Industries Association of the Philippines, organized in 1977. SPIK has grown to having 75 member firms that represent the Philippines' biggest chemical companies and major industry players.

How to Apply

Application Steps

  1. 1 Visit the company websites directly using the links provided above
  2. 2 Look for "Careers" or "Jobs" sections on their corporate websites
  3. 3 Contact the companies directly using the provided contact information
  4. 4 Check major job portals like JobStreet, Indeed Philippines, and LinkedIn
  5. 5 Attend SPIK networking events and quarterly meetings
